Cheesecake

A day of celebration

Lactose intolerant folks beware! The cheesiest cheesecake for celebrating birthdays, anniversaries, divorce, promotions and more!

Ingredients

  • Cheese Slices of Your Choice
  • Cheese Wheel of Your Choice

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lay your cheese wheel flat so that you can layer your cheese slices on top.
  2. Cover the cheese wheel from top, bottom, and on the sides with your cheese slices.
  3. Heat it up for a few seconds in the microwave or oven so that it softens and sticks well together.
  4. Done! Decorate it with candles, wording, or whatever deccoration as you please and serve!
