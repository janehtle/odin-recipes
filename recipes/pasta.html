Angel Hair Pasta

Simple, at home pasta for the kids!

Silky, delectable angel hair pasta that can be made at home in under 10 minutes. Topped with vodka sauce and "parmesan" for your tasting pleasure.

Ingredients

  • Angel Hair
  • Vodka Sauce - premade
  • Dandruff

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare a bundle of hair from an angel on a plate on the side.
  2. Pour 1 cup of Vodka sauce in a pan and heat on medium-low.
  3. Once the sauce is heated, pour the sauce on the angel hair. It will soften the strands.
  4. Garnish dandruff on top, as much as you please and serve!
