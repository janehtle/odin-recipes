Domino's Pizza

Better than DiGiorno!

Domino's Pizza easy made at home for movie nights, date nights, or a sad meal alone at home.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Domino Pieces
  • Pepperoni
  • Flour
  • Water
  • 1 Egg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, egg, and water to form the dough.
  2. Let the dough proof on the side in a nonstick bowl, cover with a towel.
  3. Once the dough proofs, roll it out in a circle and smother it with tomato sauce.
  4. Garnish mozzarella shreds, pepperoni slices, and domino pieces on top.
  5. Place in oven on nonstick pan and bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 5 minutes and serve!
